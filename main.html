About Us

About Us

Balanced Egypt is a youth-led initiative dedicated to raising awareness about food waste and its social, environmental, and economic impacts. We believe that reducing food waste is key to building a more sustainable and child-friendly urban future.

Balanced Egypt was born during the preparations for COP27, where we noticed a significant gap: while global conversations on climate change were gaining momentum, the issue of food waste was barely being addressed in Egypt. As a group of passionate young individuals, we saw this as an opportunity to make a meaningful contribution. We realized that food waste wasn’t just an environmental concern but also a pressing social issue with wide-ranging economic and health implications. Driven by this realization, we decided to take action. We began researching the issue, only to find that food waste in Egypt was not getting the attention it deserved. That’s when Balanced Egypt was born. Our mission quickly became clear: to raise awareness, educate people on the importance of reducing food waste, and implement sustainable solutions. From that moment on, we committed ourselves to addressing this issue head-on. Our goal is to lead the charge in changing how Egyptians view food, consumption, and waste, while providing practical, eco-friendly solutions that can make a lasting impact.

Partners & Collaborators

Academy of Scientific Research and Technology - ASRT

The Academy of Scientific Research and Technology (ASRT) is Egypt’s leading institution for scientific research and innovation. Since 1971, it has supported research, funded initiatives, and promoted sustainable development.

Balanced Egypt first collaborated with ASRT at the World Urban Forum (WUF) for Children at AUC to raise awareness about food waste. Building on this, we are partnering again for an event at ASRT on February 15, 2025, to educate children, teenagers, and adults on food waste and sustainability.

AUC School of Sciences and Engineering - SSE

The School of Sciences and Engineering (SSE) at the American University in Cairo (AUC) is a leading institution dedicated to advancing education and research in science, engineering, and technology. SSE focuses on innovation and tackling global challenges through academic excellence.

Balanced Egypt collaborated with SSE at the World Urban Forum (WUF) for Children, where they hosted us at their New Cairo campus to educate children about food waste. This partnership helped raise awareness of the environmental and social impacts of food waste, promoting sustainable practices among the younger generation.

Egypt Urban Campaign

The Egypt Urban Campaign is dedicated to promoting sustainable urban development and creating inclusive, child-friendly cities. Through research, advocacy, and community engagement, the campaign works to improve urban environments for future generations.

At the World Urban Forum (WUF) for Children, Balanced Egypt partnered with Egypt Urban Campaign to educate children about food waste and its impact on child-friendly urbanism. Together, we highlighted the connection between sustainable food practices and better urban living, empowering young minds to shape a greener future.

Children's University

Children's University, ASRT is a unique educational initiative by the Academy of Scientific Research and Technology (ASRT) that aims to engage and inspire young minds through hands-on learning and innovative programs. It serves as a platform for nurturing the scientific curiosity and creativity of children across Egypt.

Balanced Egypt first collaborated with Children's University at the World Urban Forum (WUF) for Children at the American University in Cairo, where we educated their students on the importance of addressing food waste. We are excited to continue this partnership with a new event on February 15, 2025, at ASRT, where we will engage students, alumni, and parents in learning about food waste and sustainability.

Global Shapers community Alexandria

The Global Shapers Community Alexandria is part of a worldwide network of young people committed to making a positive impact in their communities. Focused on leadership, social innovation, and community development, the Alexandria hub brings together passionate individuals dedicated to driving change on local and global levels.

Balanced Egypt has collaborated with the Global Shapers Community Alexandria on various projects, working together to address social and environmental issues. Through this partnership, we have exchanged members, such as Mirna Mansour, a research assistant who contributed valuable insights to our research efforts. This collaboration strengthens our shared mission to create lasting change and empower young leaders to contribute to a sustainable future.

Lifecycle of Food Waste

Published: December 2024 | By: Mariam Hossam & Amr Muhammed

Food waste is a global issue, with one-third of all food produced being wasted, impacting both food security and the environment. It occurs at every stage of the food life cycle—from production to consumption—due to inefficiencies, overproduction, poor storage, and consumer behaviors.
